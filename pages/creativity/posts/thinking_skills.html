Thinking Skills and Types of Thinking
Today we had a presentation about thinking skills & types of thinking, I took interest in logical thinking since its a skill that engineers need to develop as explained by them:
"When we say that something is logical, what we actually mean is that it makes sense. Logical reasoning skills are the ability to focus on the presented task by following the chain of the thought process by relating one statement after another, until finding the most logical conclusion."-Quoted from their document.

Why I think logical thinking is important?
Regarding to logical thinking, I strongly believe that logical thinking is an evolution of common-sense because, the more we train our common-sense the more logical it becomes.
Logical thinking lets us figure out relationships, differences and most importantly patterns. Math describes those patterns, and generalizes them in a language we all are capable of learning trough practice an training.

Conclusion
Is logical thinking fundamental? Yes, it the soul reason we are able to communicate via the internet since it was created by circuit logic, I think that each one of us should indeed atleast put an effort to train this marvelous skill.
